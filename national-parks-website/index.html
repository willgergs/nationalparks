History of National Parks

The National Park system was created to preserve America's natural beauty and wildlife for future generations.

  • 1872 - Yellowstone becomes the first National Park in the United States
  • 1916 - National Park Service established to manage parks
  • Today - Over 400 protected sites including parks, monuments, and historic areas
