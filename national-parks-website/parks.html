Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone, established in 1872, was the first national park in the United States.

  • Geysers, hot springs, and mud pots
  • Wildlife including bison, bears, and wolves
  • Grand Canyon of Yellowstone and Yellowstone Lake

Yosemite National Park

Yosemite was designated a national park in 1890 and is famous for its giant sequoias and cliffs.

  • El Capitan and Half Dome
  • Yosemite Valley waterfalls
  • Sequoia groves and diverse wildlife

Grand Canyon National Park

Grand Canyon, established in 1919, is known for its immense size and colorful landscape.

  • Colorado River and rafting
  • Scenic viewpoints along the rim
  • Hiking and desert wildlife
